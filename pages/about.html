About Tahr Gas

Tahr Medical & Industrial Gases known as TMIG saw its genesis in the year 2021, and its goal is to be a leading manufacturer and distributor of industrial, medical, and specialty gases in the Southern Region of India, with its head office and main production facility in Dindigul District, Sendurai and has established its branches in All Districts in South India to meet the demands of an extensive and diverse customer base. TMIG is equipped with state-of-art production, storage, and distribution facilities for Oxygen, Nitrogen, Argon, Acetylene, Hydrogen, Nitrous Oxide, and Carbon Dioxide. TMIG offers the most comprehensive range of gases in all purities ranging from industrial or commercial grade to ultra-high purity grades. In addition to the above, TMIG also deals in Helium, Sulfur Hexafluoride, Fluorocarbons/Refrigerants, Ammonia, Methane, Liquefied Petroleum Gases, Carbon Monoxide, Chlorine, etc. As a single-source supplier, TMIG offers the broadest range of supply options through a large distribution network.

The company will produce and market a full line of industrial and medical gases, mixture gases, with consolidated business in the trading of refrigerant gases, liquid ammonia, chlorine, welding/cutting equipment, and industrial safety products all over India & Middle East. Prime installation facilities, combined with experienced management and production personnel, have resulted in consistently high-quality products. TMIG will produce, sell and distribute atmospheric, process gases, specialty gases, and high-performance surface coatings. The products, services, and technology bring productivity and environmental benefits to a wide variety of industries, including aerospace, chemicals, electronics, energy, food and beverage, healthcare, manufacturing, metals, and others.

TMIG has high expectations to develop and expand industrial gas activities through advanced gas applications. Trading of Industrial Material and Equipment as a division of TMIG will be the stockiest / authorized distributor for some of the world-renowned, leading Manufacturers of Welding Machines, Welding consumables, accessories, Safety and Gas Control Equipment.
