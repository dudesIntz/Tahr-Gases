Oil & Gas Services

The oil and gas industry has rebounded strongly throughout 2021, with oil prices reaching their highest levels in six years.

Oil and gas services are considered to be a supportive part of the oil exploration and production companies.

We provide Oil and gas services that support the oil and gas industry, including energy exploration, transport of petroleum and gas to refineries, and the processing and delivery of energy assets to market. Our services include oil well maintenance, completion, production, supply, and logistical support services both onshore and offshore.

Pipe-line

Natural gas pipelines are generally regarded as the safest mode of energy transportation in the world today and also it is one of the most serious services that needs utmost attention as it comes with many risks.

For customers who consume very large volumes of hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen or argon each day, our pipeline supply systems may be just what you need.

Flexible and economical, Tahr’s pipeline supply is ideal for large volume and continuous-use customers located close to our pipeline operation facilities.

On-site Production

As an alternative to traditional bulk gas and liquid supplied from a nearby production plant, Tahr’s designs builds and operates a variety of on-site systems for the production of argon, nitrogen, oxygen, and hydrogen.

Our On-site plants offer a number of advantages in capital investment, good maintenance, and staffing.

Our production plants provide custom solutions for the exact purity, pressure, and flow requirements of your operations. A dedicated, uninterrupted supply of hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, and argon directly at your site delivers maximum reliability and optimization to your processes.

Small On-site Production

A high-value and very affordable solution, Tahr offers small on-site gas production in the form of “sale of gas” because Praxair builds, owns, and operates the plant at your site connected to your pipeline. You simply provide the utilities and pay for the products you use via a convenient and budget-friendly pricing plan.

Delivered fast and installed quickly, all equipment is set-up in one day (with product following shortly thereafter). In fact, all that’s required beforehand to make that happen is to have foundations and utilities in place.

Offshore/Onshore Purging

Our Multi-skilled teams offer both onshore/offshore services. If you require purging to displace the atmosphere of a process, pressure testing, and leak detection with mass spectrometry to verify equipment integrity, reactors cooled down quickly and efficiently, propelled along a pipeline for cleaning and inspection, or a nitrogen blanket to prevent air ingress and/or corrosion, we can satisfy your every need.

Fire Fighting Systems

Fire protection systems require regular servicing to run at optimum performance and effectively combat the fire.

Our service teams will regularly test and audit your system to ensure it meets all required standards. We are on call day and night throughout the year to assist with servicing, maintenance, and troubleshooting our technicians can deal with most problems on the spot.

Our broad capability and flexibility enable us to even maintain equipment supplied by other manufacturers.

Air Seperation Plants

Our company manufactures the air separation plants that create oxygen, nitrogen, and acetylene from the air via the process of compression, cooling, liquefaction, and distillation of air.

Apart from manufacturing and exporting the products, we also offer consultancy, installation, and warranty/guarantee services. Well equipped with the latest production unit, we manufacture the plants that are technologically advanced and yet cost-effective.

On-site Storage

The total value of oil, gas, and chemical terminals can be maximized when you work with the right development partner.

Our team has product storage and logistics experience ranging from early design through complete engineer-procure-construct (EPC) delivery and commissioning of facilities for marine storage and distribution of crude oil, refined products, and liquefied petroleum gas (LPG).

Gas Control Equipment

Our team has acquired years of experience in all aspects of Gas Control Equipment and is on hand to offer help and advice.

We offer services on a range of GCE Medical Equipment including oxygen concentrators together with repairs and user advice. We also offer a next-day-delivery service from our warehouse on the majority of our industrial equipment.

Cryogenic Repaire

We at Tahr offer a complete Repair, Evacuation, and Refurbishment of Cryogenic Vessels, Dura cylinders, Vacuum Insulated Transport Tanks, Vacuum Insulated Storage Tanks, and Vacuum Jacketed Pipelines.

We offer Service, Evacuate, Vacuum test and Pressure test for any model/make Vacuum containers with the help of our high-quality Workmanship, Vacuum Pumps, and Engineering Team.

Welding Equipment

We offer you to build the best setup by developing different types of welding to keep you moving forward. The Welding & Cutting Division of Tahr Gas provides the following range of high-end quality & services.

We offer the largest and most comprehensive welding range which includes machines, torches, tools, consumables, safety wear, fittings, and accessories for MIG, Stick, TIG, Spot and Oxy/Fuel welding, and Plasma.
